NOPEC 2026 – National Olympiad of Chemical Engineering

The National Olympiad of Chemical Engineering (NOPEC) 2026 is a nationwide competition organized by the Department of Chemical Engineering, Institut Teknologi Sepuluh Nopember (ITS). This olympiad is open to Senior High School / Vocational High School students (Grade 10–12) across Indonesia.
